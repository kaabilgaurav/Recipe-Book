Veggie Wrap

Healthy wrap packed with veggies and flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tortillas
  • 1 cup mixed veggies (lettuce, cucumber, tomato)
  • 1/4 cup hummus
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spread hummus over tortillas.
  2. Arrange veggies evenly.
  3. Sprinkle cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Roll up tightly.
  5. Serve chilled or lightly grilled.
