Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes perfect for mornings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp butter (melted)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Whisk until smooth batter forms.
  4. Heat pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour batter and cook until bubbles appear.
  6. Flip and cook until golden.
