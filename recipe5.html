Cookies

Crispy and chewy sweet treats.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugars together.
  3. Add egg and vanilla; mix well.
  4. Mix in dry ingredients.
