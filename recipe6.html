Veg Burger

Juicy burger with a veggie patty.

Ingredients:

  • 2 burger buns
  • 2 veg patties
  • 2 slices cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion rings
  • Mayonnaise and ketchup

Instructions:

  1. Toast buns lightly.
  2. Cook patties until golden.
  3. Place lettuce, tomato, onion on bun base.
  4. Add patty and cheese slice.
  5. Spread sauces and cover with top bun.
