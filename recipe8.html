Grilled Sandwich

Grilled bread with cheesy veggie filling.

Ingredients:

  • 4 bread slices
  • 1 cup mixed veggies (capsicum, onion, tomato)
  • 2 slices cheese
  • Butter for grilling
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix veggies with salt and pepper.
  2. Spread mixture on bread.
  3. Add cheese slice on top.
  4. Cover with another bread slice.
  5. Apply butter and grill until golden.
