Creamy Pasta

Pasta in creamy white sauce with veggies.

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil pasta per instructions and drain.
  2. SautÃ© garlic and vegetables in butter.
  3. Add milk and cream, cook until thickened.
  4. Add pasta and toss well.
  5. Season and serve hot.
