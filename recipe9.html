Margherita Pizza

Classic Italian pizza with tomato and basil.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Spread sauce over pizza base.
  2. Top with mozzarella and basil.
  3. Drizzle olive oil.
  4. Bake at 200Â°C for 10-12 min.
