Chocolate Cake

Rich and moist chocolate indulgence.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix well.
  4. Pour batter into greased pan.
  5. Bake 30-35 min until toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool and frost as desired.
