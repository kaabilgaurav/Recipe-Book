Fresh Salad

A refreshing and healthy green salad.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place greens in a large bowl.
  2. Add tomato, cucumber, and feta.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss gently and serve fresh.
