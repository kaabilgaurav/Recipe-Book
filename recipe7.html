Smoothie Bowl

Colorful bowl with fruits and seeds.

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup frozen berries
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • Fresh fruits for topping
  • Chia seeds

Instructions:

  1. Blend banana, berries, and yogurt until smooth.
  2. Pour into bowl.
  3. Top with granola, fresh fruits, and chia seeds.
  4. Serve chilled.
