Veg Biryani

Fragrant rice cooked with spices and vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice (cooked)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and saut√© onions till golden.
  2. Add vegetables and cook for 5 min.
  3. Sprinkle biryani masala and mix well.
  4. Add cooked rice and gently combine.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
