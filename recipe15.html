Veg Tacos

Crunchy tacos filled with fresh vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 4 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked beans
  • Salsa or sauce as desired

Instructions:

  1. Warm taco shells as per packet instructions.
  2. Fill with lettuce, beans, tomatoes.
  3. Add grated cheese on top.
  4. Drizzle salsa or sauce.
  5. Serve immediately.
