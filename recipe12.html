Veg Fried Rice

Quick stir-fried rice with veggies and spices.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup chopped vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in pan.
  2. Saut√© vegetables till tender.
  3. Add cooked rice and soy sauce.
  4. Mix well and cook 3-4 min.
  5. Season and serve hot.
